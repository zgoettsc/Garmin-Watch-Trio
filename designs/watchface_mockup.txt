COLOR SCHEME (64-color MIP palette):
─────────────────────────────────────────

    Element        Color              Rationale
    ───────        ─────              ─────────
    Background     Black (0x000000)   Battery efficient on MIP
    Time           White (0xFFFFFF)   Maximum readability
    Date           Light gray         Visible but not competing
    BG value       Dynamic:
                     Green            In range (e.g. 70-180)
                     Yellow           Caution (e.g. 55-70, 180-250)
                     Red              Urgent (e.g. <55, >250)
    Trend arrow    Same as BG color   Reinforces urgency
    Delta          White              Clean, neutral
    IOB label      Cyan/light blue    Distinct from BG colors
    COB label      Orange/amber       Distinct, food-associated
    Loop dot       Green (active)     Intuitive "good" signal
    Loop X         Red (inactive)     Intuitive "warning" signal
